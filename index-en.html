About Us

Cherry Puzzles

Cherry Puzzles came into existence as an idea which aims to combine the pleasure of putting pieces of a jigsaw together with the joy of experiencing a visually pleasing work of art.

Every single one of the 500 pieces of jigsaw in the box is waiting for you and your friends to put them together with their perfectly compatible matches.

From İzmir with Love.

Have fun and good luck!

WHY

CHERRY PUZZLES

Superior Quality

All Cherry Puzzles products are manufactured with quality materials and workmanship. So you can be sure that each unique piece can be put together perfectly.

Unique Design

Different, modern and unique. Cherry Puzzles offers a dynamic experience different from traditional jigsaw puzzles with the visuals that you will want to frame and display after you are finished, and the box designs that possess collectible value.

Women's Initiative

We are proud of the mission of the Cherry Puzzles project, a women's initiative, to make a positive contribution to the quality time spent at home and to change the perception of the boring puzzles that we are tired of seeing.
